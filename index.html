A tribute to Fran Kirby

Introduction

Francesca (Fran) Kirby, Chelsea's prolific number 14, has cemented herself as one of Chelsea Women's most iconic and lethal players at the club. This tribute to Fran showcases her talent and strength; some of the many reasons as to why she is revered as one of the greats in Women's football. Instead of choosing to shed light on one of Chelsea's male footballers, I chose Fran as the subject of this tribute instead, hoping to challenge my own instincts when it comes to showing appreciation of the incredible amount of talent seen at the club I support.

Appreciation

One of the reasons I chose Fran in particular was not only because of her footballing ability, but due to her triumphant battle with the rare condition pericarditis. The condition is known to cause inflammation of the pericardium -- a fluid filled sac that covers the heart, which in turn meant, she was to be told by medics that she would not be able to play football again. Despite the a grave outlook to her football career, Fran had not only fought the odds to make a full recovery and comeback to football, but playing at a level that saw her recognised as the top performer at the club, becoming Chelsea's all-time goalscorer and playing a crucial part in the many trophies that followed.
